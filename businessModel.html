BUSINESS MODEL

We team with our clients: we are not an outsourcing organization, instead, we extend our client teams.

  1. We virtualize our client workflows by tightly integrating client processes, policies and protocols.
  2. We strive for operational excellence and bring forward named, dedicated, experienced associates, who collaborate with our client teams.

We strive for operational excellence and client confidence.

  1. We bring forward a world-class, digital, paper-free production infrastructure.
  2. We do not download or store patient data.
  3. We complete work in client systems, and our work quality and productivity are fully transparent to our clients.

We endeavor to fail fast, learn faster and grow.

  1. We deliver on our commitments, differentiate on performance, and exceed customer expectations.
  2. We are inclusive, collaborative and selfless in seeking the best outcomes for our clients.

We believe in technology leverage and continued relevancy through constant dis-intermediation.

  1. We collaborate with our clients and technology partners to share productivity, feature and automation gains (RPA, IPA, AI, ML, etc.).
  2. We perform quarterly contract reviews and reset SLAs to constantly dis-intermediate our services — and refocus on energy on services that are of higher value to our clients — to keep us relevant.
